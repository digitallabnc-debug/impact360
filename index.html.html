Our Mission

To connect youth to opportunities, shape positive life outcomes, and drive community impact through skills, behavior change, and climate action.

Our Vision

A generation of empowered, responsible, and opportunity-driven youth transforming Tharaka Nithi County — ward by ward.

Our Programs

Three pillars.
One purpose.

Each program is anchored to a verified institutional partner and designed to produce outcomes that are visible, measurable, and community-owned.

01
Life360
Behavior & Life Outcomes · Mindset · Discipline

Equipping youth with tools to make responsible life decisions — from substance abuse prevention to mental health and peer leadership.

  • Anti-drug & substance abuse campaigns
  • School and campus outreach talks
  • Mental health and life skills sessions
  • Peer mentorship programs
Verified Partner
NACADA
National Authority for Campaign Against Alcohol & Drug Abuse
02
Green360
Environment & Climate Action · Conservation · Sustainability

Mobilizing youth to lead environmental protection and climate resilience across Tharaka Nithi — from tree planting to community conservation.

  • Monthly tree planting drives
  • Environmental clean-up campaigns
  • Climate awareness workshops
  • Community conservation projects
Verified Partners
Kenya Forest Service
Kenya Wildlife Service
03
Opportunity360
Skills & Economic Development · Employment · Entrepreneurship

Bridging the gap between youth and income — connecting young people to internships, digital skills, and real entrepreneurship pathways.

  • Weekly jobs, tenders & internship alerts
  • Digital skills bootcamps
  • University partnership programs
  • Youth entrepreneurship support
Verified Partners
Tharaka University
