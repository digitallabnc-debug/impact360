What We Do

Three Pillars.
One Mission.

Each program is anchored to a real institutional partner — credible, trackable, and fundable from day one.

01

Life 360

Mental health awareness, substance abuse prevention, and life skills training for youth in schools and community spaces. Delivered in partnership with Kenya's lead drug abuse authority.

🏛 NACADA
02

Green 360

Environmental stewardship, tree planting, and conservation action programs engaging youth in protecting Tharaka Nithi's natural resources. In collaboration with government forest and wildlife agencies.

🌳 KFS · KWS
03

Opportunity 360

Skills development, mentorship, internship pipelines, and economic empowerment connecting young people to real opportunities through university and industry partners.
